Why do you need a paper wallet?

To keep your Bitcoins safe, you need a secure wallet. Exchanges and online wallets are always connected to the internet. Therefore, they are not suitable for storing cryptocurrencies on the long term. Maintaining a constant online connection will get your wallet exposed to hacker attacks. On the other hand, paper wallets offer cold storage (no connection to the internet), which is considered as one of the safest ways to store your Bitcoins. Paper wallets for cryptos have the same functionality as pocket wallets for cash. While both of them offer storage for your money, as they are physical, they can be lost or stolen. To avoid that, you have to store your paper wallet in a safe location where you have exclusive access to.

How to Use Our Paper Wallet

How to keep it safe?

You don’t want anyone to access your Bitcoins, that’s why you have to keep your paper wallet in a safe place. As it is a cold storage, you don’t have to worry about hackers, only about the location you store your paper wallet. You should choose a place in your home that you exclusively have access to. This can be a safe deposit box, a hidden place in your room, a cabinet, or even a specific page of your favorite book. You should also keep your paper wallet in a zip bag or a box where water or other liquids can’t damage it.

How to load funds to the wallet?

First, you need a “normal” Bitcoin wallet (either an online, exchange or software wallet), which you will use to load your paper wallet. We recommend using our wallet as you can buy Bitcoin in there with your credit card. After you have the desired amount of Bitcoins in your “normal” wallet, you can deposit funds to the paper wallet by scanning the wallet’s public address and sending Bitcoins there.

How to verify how much is on it?

You can verify your Bitcoin balance by using our block explorer and scanning the public address on your paper wallet (we recommend using your phone’s camera for that). You can scan your address by clicking the small QR code icon right next to the search bar. You can also verify your balance by typing in your public key to the search bar.

How to withdraw funds?

To withdraw funds from your paper wallet, you should download our digital wallet and scan the private key inside the application. For security reasons, if you withdraw funds from your paper wallet, you should withdraw ALL of your balance at once. Don’t leave any funds in your paper wallet, as it could get lost. NEVER use the same paper wallet to deposit funds again! You should create a new one instead.

Paper wallet generation best practices:

Start our generator and save the page with your browser. You can do that by right-clicking anywhere on the site and hitting “Save as” or navigating in “File” and clicking on “Save as”. The next step is to disconnect from the internet, open the page from the file you just saved, and generate your private and public keys. Before printing your wallet, make sure your printer is not connected to the internet. After you are done printing, you should delete all files connected to your paper wallet from your computer, clear your browser cache, and reboot your printer (you can also clear the printer’s memory). After that, you can go online and back to business as usual!
